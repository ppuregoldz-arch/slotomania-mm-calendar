## Business Strategy Insights

### Rolling Offer Positioning
1. **High-frequency launches** - Nearly daily activity vs Prize Mania's ~10-day gaps
2. **Segmented approach** - Targets different PU volumes and ARPPUs per date
3. **Premium monetization** - Higher ARPPUs suggest more valuable offer content
4. **Flexible scaling** - Can run both volume (10K+ PUs) and premium (3K-5K PUs) campaigns

### Launch Patterns
- **May focus**: 15+ launch dates in May alone
- **Variable intensity**: Some dates optimize for volume, others for revenue per user
- **Test/rollout approach**: Minimal activity dates (May 20) suggest controlled testing

The Rolling Offer strategy appears more dynamic and segmented compared to Prize Mania's consistent volume approach.
