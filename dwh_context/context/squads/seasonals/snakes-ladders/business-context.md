# Snakes & Ladders - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Snakes & Ladders** is a seasonal feature based on the classic Chutes and Ladders board game, originally introduced in 2017 and remastered in 2026 with enhanced rewards and gameplay. Players progress through a 5-board system using dice rolls, encounter snakes (slides down) and ladders (climb up), and can purchase gem-based boosters for enhanced gameplay.

## Core Mechanics

### Dice Roll Economy
- **Acquisition Methods**: Dice rolls obtained through bet-based bar filling and special offer purchases
- **Bar Progression**: Higher bets fill progress bar faster (player-specific calibration similar to Blast)
- **Storage System**: Purchased dice rolls stored in Stash, earned dice rolls reset with season
- **Unlimited Earning**: Theoretical unlimited dice rolls through continued bar filling

### Board Progression System
- **5-Board Structure**: Players progress through 5 distinct boards toward Final Prize
- **Starting Position**: All players begin at position "zero" on first board
- **Movement Mechanics**: Dice roll determines number of cells moved forward
- **Snake Effects**: Landing on snake head causes slide down to lower position
- **Ladder Benefits**: Landing at ladder base allows climb up to higher position
- **Final Prize**: Typically Wild Card reward for reaching Final Board completion

### Interactive Elements
- **Green Bonus Cards**: Special landing spaces providing randomized rewards:
  - Coin gifts for immediate currency boost
  - Extra dice rolls for additional attempts
  - Bigger board completion prizes for enhanced rewards
- **Auto Roll Functionality**: Long press Roll button for 3 seconds to activate automatic rolling
- **Board Restart**: Upon completion, feature restarts with 5-star card pack reward

## Monetization & Boosters

### Gem-Based Boosters
- **Multi-Wheel**: Multiplies dice roll results with additional gem/coin rewards from wheel spin
- **Persistent Shield**: Protects against snake effects once before breaking
- **Booster Restrictions**: Cannot purchase boosters during auto-roll mode
- **Strategic Usage**: Players must choose when to invest gems for maximum progression benefit

### Purchase Integration
- **Special Offers**: Dice rolls available through targeted purchase offers
- **Gem Integration**: Booster purchases integrated with existing gem economy
- **Stash Storage**: Purchased dice rolls preserved across seasonal resets
- **Value Proposition**: Enhanced progression and protection justify gem investment

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Nostalgic Appeal**: Classic board game mechanics create familiar and comfortable engagement
- **Strategic Depth**: Risk-reward decisions around booster usage and progression timing
- **Completion Drive**: 5-board progression with final Wild Card prize motivates sustained engagement
- **Monetization Integration**: Boosters and dice roll purchases provide clear value propositions

### Strategic Value
- **Extended Engagement**: Multi-board progression extends play sessions and seasonal participation
- **Familiar Mechanics**: Board game foundation reduces learning curve while providing depth
- **Gem Economy Contribution**: Booster purchases provide consistent gem spending opportunities
- **Cross-Feature Rewards**: Wild Cards and card packs integrate with album collection system

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Progression Metrics
- **Board Advancement Rate**: Average progression speed through 5-board system
- **Completion Achievement**: % of participants reaching Final Board and prize
- **Board Drop-off Analysis**: Where players typically stop progressing through boards
- **Restart Engagement**: % of completers who restart feature for additional rewards

### Dice Roll Economy
- **Earning vs Purchase Ratio**: Balance between earned dice rolls and purchased dice rolls
- **Bar Fill Efficiency**: Bet amounts and frequency required for dice roll generation
- **Storage Utilization**: How players manage limited dice roll inventory
- **Cross-Seasonal Retention**: Purchased dice rolls impact on seasonal re-engagement

### Booster Performance
- **Multi-Wheel Usage**: Purchase frequency and effectiveness of dice multiplier booster
- **Shield Deployment**: When and how often players purchase snake protection
- **Gem ROI**: Booster investment impact on progression success and Final Prize achievement
- **Auto Roll Impact**: Auto roll usage effect on booster purchase decisions

### Board Game Analytics
- **Snake Encounter Rate**: Frequency and impact of snake effects on progression
- **Ladder Utilization**: How often players benefit from ladder advancement opportunities
- **Green Bonus Distribution**: Bonus card reward distribution and player satisfaction
- **Position Distribution**: Player position spread across boards and progression stages

## Technical Architecture

### Board State Management
- **Multi-Board Tracking**: Individual progress across 5 distinct board layouts
- **Position Persistence**: Player position saved across sessions until completion or reset
- **Snake/Ladder Logic**: Automated position adjustment based on landing space effects
- **Green Bonus Processing**: Random reward selection and delivery from bonus card landings

### Dice Roll Systems
- **Bar Progression Tracking**: Bet-based meter filling calibrated per player
- **Inventory Management**: Separate tracking for earned vs purchased dice rolls
- **Auto Roll State**: Mode management preventing booster purchases during automatic play
- **Cross-Seasonal Storage**: Purchased dice rolls preserved across seasonal boundaries

## Feature Integration

### Seasonal Framework Integration
- **5-7 Day Cycles**: Snakes & Ladders fits standard seasonal rotation schedule
- **Album Theme Alignment**: Board aesthetics and rewards coordinate with current album themes
- **Final Prize Integration**: Wild Card rewards support album collection progression
- **Cross-Seasonal Balance**: SNL monetization coordinated with other concurrent seasonals

### Reward System Integration
- **Wild Card Distribution**: Final Prize Wild Cards integrated with album completion systems
- **Card Pack Rewards**: 5-star card packs delivered through existing reward systems
- **Coin Integration**: Green bonus coin gifts balanced with game economy health
- **Cross-Feature Benefits**: SNL completion potentially affecting other game area rewards

## Risk Areas & Monitoring

### Board Game Balance
- **Progression Difficulty**: Ensure 5-board progression challenging but achievable for engaged players
- **Snake/Ladder Distribution**: Balance negative snake effects with positive ladder opportunities
- **Green Bonus Frequency**: Monitor bonus card encounter rates for player satisfaction

### Monetization Health
- **Booster Value Perception**: Multi-Wheel and Shield boosters must provide clear progression benefits
- **Purchase Pressure**: Balance gem monetization with positive free-to-play experience
- **Dice Roll Economy**: Maintain sustainable balance between earning and purchasing dice rolls

### Player Experience
- **Nostalgic Authenticity**: Preserve classic board game feel while adding modern enhancements
- **Auto Roll Balance**: Ensure automatic play remains engaging without eliminating strategic decisions
- **Completion Satisfaction**: Final Prize and restart rewards must justify progression investment
