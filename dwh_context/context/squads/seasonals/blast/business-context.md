# Blast - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Blast** is a seasonal feature owned by Aviv that centers on uncovering blast symbols to advance through boards or levels. The primary monetization comes through extra pick upsells, allowing players additional uncovering attempts when they run out of free picks or want to maximize their progression opportunities.

## Core Mechanics

### Blast Uncovering System
- **Blast Symbol Discovery**: Players uncover tiles/spaces to find blast symbols that enable progression
- **Board/Level Advancement**: Finding blast symbols allows movement to next board or deeper level
- **Limited Attempts**: Players have finite picks/attempts per entry, creating scarcity and urgency
- **Dead End Avoidance**: Strategic uncovering to avoid wasting picks on empty or blocking tiles

### Extra Pick Monetization
- **Primary Revenue Driver**: Extra pick purchases when players exhaust free attempts
- **Urgency Creation**: Limited free picks create immediate purchase motivation
- **Progressive Value**: Deeper levels may offer better rewards, justifying extra pick investment
- **Multiple Purchase Opportunities**: Players can buy extra picks multiple times per session

### Progression Structure
- **Board-Based Progression**: Advancement through series of boards with increasing complexity
- **Escalating Rewards**: Deeper progression offers more valuable rewards and bonuses
- **Strategic Depth**: Players must balance pick usage with progression goals
- **Completion Incentives**: Full board/level completion provides significant reward bonuses

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Direct Monetization**: Extra pick sales drive immediate revenue during seasonal periods
- **Short-Term Engagement**: 5-7 day seasonal cycle creates focused engagement spikes
- **Strategic Gameplay**: Uncovering decisions create engaging risk-reward gameplay
- **Repeat Participation**: Multiple boards and escalating rewards encourage repeated play

### Strategic Value
- **Revenue Concentration**: Focused monetization opportunity during seasonal launch periods
- **Player Excitement**: Blast discovery creates satisfying progression moments
- **Purchase Justification**: Clear value proposition for extra pick investments
- **Content Freshness**: Rotating seasonal provides regular new content experiences

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Core Engagement Metrics
- **Board Entry Rate**: % of eligible players who enter Blast seasonal
- **Progression Depth**: Average number of boards/levels completed per participant
- **Pick Usage Efficiency**: Average picks used per board completion
- **Session Length**: Time spent per Blast gameplay session

### Monetization Metrics
- **Extra Pick Conversion**: % of participants who purchase additional attempts
- **Revenue per Participant**: Average spending per engaged Blast player
- **Purchase Frequency**: Number of extra pick purchases per player per session
- **Revenue per Pick**: Average revenue generated per extra pick sold

### Progression Analytics
- **Board Completion Rates**: Success rates for each board/level in progression
- **Drop-off Analysis**: Where players typically stop progressing through boards
- **Reward Effectiveness**: Impact of rewards on continued progression and engagement
- **Difficulty Calibration**: Balance between challenge and achievable progression

## Risk Areas & Monitoring

### Monetization Balance
- **Purchase Pressure**: Balance extra pick pricing with positive player experience
- **Free Progression**: Ensure viable path for players who don't purchase extra picks
- **Value Perception**: Extra picks must provide clear and satisfying value proposition

### Gameplay Health
- **Difficulty Progression**: Board difficulty must escalate appropriately without frustrating players
- **Pick Economy Balance**: Free pick allocation balanced with monetization opportunities
- **Completion Achievability**: Ensure skilled/dedicated players can achieve meaningful progression

### Player Experience
- **Frustration Prevention**: Avoid creating overly punishing or unfair uncovering experiences
- **Strategic Depth**: Maintain engaging decision-making without overwhelming complexity
- **Reward Satisfaction**: Blast progression rewards must justify time and potential spending investment
