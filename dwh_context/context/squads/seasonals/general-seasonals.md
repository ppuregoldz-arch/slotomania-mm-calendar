# General Seasonal System - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Seasonal Features** are short-term rotating surprise content (5-7 days) themed by month/album cycles that create excitement through novelty and discovery. Most seasonals require meter filling through bet or win-based activities to enter, and many feature direct-revenue products like extra picks, driving both engagement and incremental monetization.

## Core Seasonal Framework

### Duration & Rotation
- **Short-Term Cycles**: Typically 5-7 days per seasonal feature
- **Rotating Content**: Regular rotation prevents feature fatigue and maintains surprise elements
- **Themed Alignment**: Seasonal content aligned with monthly themes and current album seasons
- **Surprise Discovery**: Players encounter new mechanics and themes regularly

### Entry Mechanics
- **Meter-Fill Requirements**: Most seasonals require filling a progress meter to gain entry
- **Bet-Based Progression**: Meter filling typically driven by betting activity on designated machines
- **Win-Based Progression**: Some seasonals progress through win achievements or amounts
- **Entry Threshold**: Once meter is filled, players gain access to seasonal minigame or feature

### Direct Revenue Products
- **Extra Picks**: Primary monetization through additional game attempts or chances
- **Premium Progression**: Accelerated advancement through direct purchases
- **Special Items**: Feature-specific items enhancing gameplay or rewards
- **Gem Integration**: Seasonal-specific gem spending opportunities

## Key Seasonal Examples

### Blast (Owner: Aviv)
- **Core Mechanic**: Uncover blast symbols to advance through boards/levels
- **Primary Revenue**: Extra pick upsells allowing additional uncovering attempts
- **Progression**: Advance by finding blast symbols, avoid dead ends or blockers
- **Reward Structure**: Escalating rewards for deeper progression into boards

### Battle Sheep (Owner: Aviv)
- **Core Mechanic**: Uncover clouds revealing different outcomes (empty/ship/jackpot/wolf)
- **Progression System**: Parasheeps gained through purchase or spinning to enhance progression
- **Multiple Outcomes**: Varied results create excitement and strategic decision-making
- **Risk-Reward**: Balance between safe progression and high-reward risky uncovering

### Pick-Based Seasonals
- **Universal Mechanic**: Pick-one reveal mechanics across multiple seasonal themes
- **Extra Pick Monetization**: Additional attempts through gem or direct purchase
- **Variety in Themes**: Different seasonal themes using similar pick-reveal mechanics
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Later picks often require more entry requirements or cost more

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Content Freshness**: Regular new content preventing player engagement fatigue
- **Surprise & Discovery**: Rotating mechanics maintain game excitement and unpredictability  
- **Incremental Revenue**: Direct monetization through extra picks and premium items
- **Short-Term Goals**: Weekly completion objectives providing immediate play motivation
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Seasonal rewards support other game systems (cards, gems, coins)

### Strategic Value
- **Engagement Spikes**: Seasonal launches create temporary engagement increases
- **Revenue Diversification**: Additional monetization streams beyond core features
- **Content Testing**: Seasonal features serve as testing ground for new mechanics
- **Player Retention**: Regular content updates maintain long-term player interest
- **Themed Cohesion**: Album-aligned seasonal content creates unified game experience

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Engagement Metrics
- **Entry Rate**: % of active players who fill meters and enter seasonal features
- **Participation Depth**: Average progression depth per participating player
- **Completion Rate**: % of entrants who complete or significantly progress through seasonal
- **Multi-Entry Rate**: Players who re-enter or replay seasonal features multiple times

### Monetization Metrics
- **Extra Pick Conversion**: % of participants who purchase additional attempts
- **Revenue per Participant**: Average direct spending within seasonal features
- **Gem Usage**: Seasonal-specific gem spending patterns and amounts
- **Purchase Timing**: When during seasonal cycles players make purchases

### Content Performance
- **Feature Appeal**: Participation rates across different seasonal themes and mechanics
- **Progression Distribution**: Player advancement patterns through seasonal content
- **Drop-off Points**: Where players typically stop engaging with seasonal features
- **Cross-Feature Impact**: Seasonal participation effect on other game areas

## Technical Architecture

### Core Data Systems
- **Event Tracking**: Detailed logging of seasonal entry, progression, and completion
- **Revenue Attribution**: Clear tracking of seasonal-specific purchases and gem spending  
- **Meter Progression**: Bet/win-based meter filling tracking and threshold management
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Seasonal rewards distribution to other game systems

### Content Management
- **Rotation Scheduling**: Systematic deployment and rotation of seasonal content
- **Theme Coordination**: Alignment with album seasons and monthly themes
- **A/B Testing**: Seasonal mechanic and monetization optimization
- **Performance Monitoring**: Real-time engagement and revenue tracking

### Integration Systems
- **Product Integration**: Seasonal-specific SKUs and purchase options
- **Reward Distribution**: Cross-feature reward delivery (cards, gems, coins)
- **Entry Gate Management**: Meter-fill threshold and entry requirement systems
- **Analytics Integration**: Comprehensive performance tracking and reporting

## Feature Ecosystem Integration

### Album System Integration
- **Themed Alignment**: Seasonal content matches current album themes and aesthetics
- **Card Rewards**: Seasonal completion often grants album cards or collection benefits
- **Cross-Promotion**: Album progression encouraging seasonal participation

### Daily Feature Integration
- **Complementary Scheduling**: Seasonal features coordinate with Daily Dash and other daily content
- **Reward Synergy**: Seasonal rewards enhance daily progression and vice versa
- **Engagement Distribution**: Balance seasonal excitement with daily habit formation

### Monetization Integration
- **Purchase Tools**: Seasonal-specific personal offers and purchase opportunities
- **Gem Economy**: Seasonal gem spending opportunities supporting overall gem economy
- **Revenue Coordination**: Seasonal monetization complementing rather than competing with core revenue

## Risk Areas & Monitoring

### Content Balance
- **Feature Fatigue**: Monitor player response to seasonal rotation frequency
- **Difficulty Calibration**: Balance seasonal entry requirements and progression challenge
- **Revenue Pressure**: Avoid aggressive monetization that damages player experience

### System Integration
- **Cross-Feature Harmony**: Ensure seasonal features enhance rather than disrupt core gameplay
- **Technical Stability**: Monitor seasonal feature performance and system integration
- **Content Pipeline**: Maintain consistent seasonal content development and deployment

### Player Experience
- **Surprise vs. Predictability**: Balance novelty with player understanding and comfort
- **Accessibility**: Ensure seasonal features remain accessible to different player segments
- **Completion Satisfaction**: Seasonal progression and rewards must justify player investment
