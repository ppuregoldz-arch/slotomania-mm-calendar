# Pick-Based Seasonals - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Pick-Based Seasonals** represent a category of seasonal features that utilize pick-one reveal mechanics across various themed content. These features share common extra pick monetization strategies while offering diverse themes and outcomes, providing consistent gameplay familiarity with fresh seasonal variety.

## Core Mechanics

### Universal Pick System
- **Pick-One Reveals**: Players select from available options to reveal outcomes
- **Limited Free Picks**: Standard allocation of free attempts per entry or session
- **Extra Pick Purchases**: Additional attempts available through gem spending or direct purchase
- **Progressive Revelation**: Pick results often unlock additional pick opportunities or advancement

### Pick Economy Framework
- **Picks In**: Token collection through gameplay, rewards, or entry requirements
- **Picks Out**: Pick usage through gameplay attempts and reveal actions
- **Pick Balance**: System monitoring pick generation vs consumption across seasonal features
- **Source Attribution**: Tracking earned vs purchased pick origins and effectiveness

### Themed Variations
- **Seasonal Theme Integration**: Pick mechanics adapted to various monthly/album themes
- **Outcome Diversity**: Different rewards and progression based on seasonal theme
- **Visual Variety**: Fresh presentation while maintaining familiar pick-based gameplay
- **Mechanic Consistency**: Core pick system remains consistent across seasonal variations

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Monetization Consistency**: Reliable extra pick revenue across different seasonal themes
- **Player Familiarity**: Consistent mechanics reduce learning curve for new seasonal content
- **Theme Flexibility**: Pick system adapts to various seasonal themes and narratives
- **Revenue Predictability**: Established pick-based monetization provides forecasting reliability

### Strategic Value
- **Content Efficiency**: Proven pick mechanics allow focus on theme and presentation variety
- **Player Comfort**: Familiar gameplay reduces friction in seasonal feature adoption
- **Monetization Optimization**: Refined extra pick pricing and timing based on accumulated data
- **Cross-Seasonal Learning**: Performance insights transfer across pick-based seasonal features

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Pick Economy Metrics
- **Pick Flow Balance**: Monitoring pick generation vs usage across all pick-based seasonals
- **Source Effectiveness**: Comparing earned vs purchased pick conversion and satisfaction
- **Usage Patterns**: How players deploy picks strategically across different seasonal themes
- **Economy Health**: Overall pick system sustainability and player satisfaction

### Monetization Performance
- **Extra Pick Conversion**: % of participants purchasing additional picks across seasonal themes
- **Revenue per Pick**: Average revenue generated per extra pick sold by seasonal theme
- **Purchase Timing**: When during seasonal cycles players buy extra picks
- **Theme Revenue Comparison**: Monetization effectiveness across different pick-based seasonal themes

### Cross-Seasonal Analytics
- **Theme Appeal**: Participation and engagement rates across different pick-based themes
- **Mechanic Refinement**: How pick system improvements affect performance across seasons
- **Player Retention**: Pick-based seasonal participation impact on overall player retention
- **Feature Evolution**: How pick-based mechanics evolve and improve over seasonal cycles
