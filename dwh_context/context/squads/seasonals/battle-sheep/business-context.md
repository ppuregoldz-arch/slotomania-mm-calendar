# Battle Sheep - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Battle Sheep** is a seasonal feature owned by Aviv featuring cloud uncovering mechanics with multiple outcome types (empty/ship/jackpot/wolf). Players progress through parasheep acquisition from purchases or spinning, creating a strategic uncovering experience with varied risk-reward outcomes and direct monetization opportunities.

## Core Mechanics

### Cloud Uncovering System
- **Multiple Outcome Types**: Clouds reveal different results (empty, ship, jackpot, wolf) with varying values
- **Strategic Uncovering**: Players must choose which clouds to reveal with limited attempts
- **Outcome Variability**: Different cloud contents create excitement and unpredictability
- **Risk-Reward Balance**: Players balance safe progression vs high-reward risky choices

### Parasheep Progression System
- **Parasheep Currency**: Special tokens earned through purchase or spinning activities
- **Progression Enhancement**: Parasheeps provide advantages in cloud uncovering or board advancement
- **Multiple Acquisition**: Players can earn parasheeps through gameplay or buy them directly
- **Strategic Resource**: Parasheeps become strategic resource for optimizing cloud uncovering

### Outcome Structure
- **Empty Clouds**: No immediate reward but may provide progression or information
- **Ship Outcomes**: Medium-value rewards with potential bonus effects
- **Jackpot Results**: High-value outcomes providing significant coin or reward bonuses
- **Wolf Encounters**: Special outcomes with unique mechanics or risk/reward dynamics

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Direct Monetization**: Parasheep purchases and extra picks drive immediate revenue
- **Strategic Engagement**: Multiple outcome types create engaging decision-making gameplay
- **Risk-Reward Excitement**: Varied outcomes maintain player interest and replay value
- **Resource Management**: Parasheep economy creates strategic depth and spending motivation

### Strategic Value
- **Gameplay Sophistication**: Multiple mechanics (uncovering + parasheeps + outcomes) create rich experience
- **Monetization Layers**: Both parasheep purchases and extra picks provide revenue opportunities
- **Replay Value**: Outcome variability encourages multiple play sessions
- **Player Investment**: Strategic resource management increases player investment and engagement

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Core Engagement Metrics
- **Cloud Uncovering Rate**: Average clouds revealed per gameplay session
- **Outcome Distribution**: Player encounter rates with different cloud contents
- **Session Depth**: Average progression depth per Battle Sheep session
- **Replay Frequency**: How often players re-enter Battle Sheep during seasonal period

### Parasheep Economy Metrics
- **Parasheep Acquisition Rate**: Earning rate through spinning vs purchase
- **Parasheep Usage Patterns**: How players deploy parasheeps strategically
- **Purchase Conversion**: % of players who buy parasheeps vs earning them
- **Resource Efficiency**: Parasheep effectiveness in improving outcomes

### Outcome Performance
- **Jackpot Hit Rate**: Frequency and value of jackpot cloud discoveries
- **Ship Outcome Value**: Average rewards from ship cloud uncovering
- **Wolf Encounter Impact**: Effect of wolf outcomes on player progression and satisfaction
- **Empty Cloud Strategy**: How empty clouds affect player decision-making and progression

### Monetization Metrics
- **Parasheep Revenue**: Direct purchase revenue from parasheep sales
- **Extra Pick Revenue**: Additional revenue from pick purchases for more uncovering
- **Revenue per Participant**: Average spending per engaged Battle Sheep player
- **Purchase Timing**: When during sessions players make parasheep or pick purchases

## Technical Architecture

### Cloud & Outcome System
- **Outcome Probability Management**: Balancing frequencies of empty/ship/jackpot/wolf results
- **Cloud State Tracking**: Recording uncovered vs unrevealed clouds per session
- **Reward Distribution**: Delivering outcome-based rewards and bonuses
- **Progression Integration**: Tracking advancement through cloud uncovering success

### Parasheep Economy
- **Resource Tracking**: Monitoring parasheep earning, purchasing, and usage
- **Acquisition Attribution**: Distinguishing earned vs purchased parasheeps
- **Strategic Usage**: Recording how players deploy parasheeps for advantages
- **Balance Management**: Ensuring parasheep economy remains balanced and engaging

## Feature Integration

### Seasonal Framework Integration
- **Battle Sheep Positioning**: Primary seasonal feature with comprehensive analytics support
- **5-7 Day Cycles**: Fits standard seasonal duration with potential for themed variations
- **Cross-Seasonal Balance**: Battle Sheep monetization coordinated with other seasonal features
- **Entry Mechanics**: Meter-fill or other requirements preceding Battle Sheep access

### Revenue Integration
- **Gem Economy**: Battle Sheep gem spending integrated with overall gem economy health
- **Purchase Integration**: Parasheep and extra pick purchases through existing payment systems
- **Revenue Attribution**: Clear tracking of Battle Sheep-specific spending and revenue

## Risk Areas & Monitoring

### Outcome Balance
- **Probability Calibration**: Ensure fair and satisfying distribution of outcome types
- **Jackpot Frequency**: Balance jackpot excitement with economic sustainability
- **Frustration Prevention**: Avoid too many empty clouds creating negative player experience

### Parasheep Economy Health
- **Earning vs Purchase Balance**: Maintain viable earning path while encouraging purchases
- **Strategic Value**: Parasheeps must provide clear and meaningful gameplay advantages
- **Resource Inflation**: Monitor parasheep economy for balance and value preservation

### Player Experience
- **Decision Complexity**: Balance strategic depth with accessibility for casual players
- **Outcome Satisfaction**: All outcome types must provide appropriate value or advancement
- **Risk-Reward Perception**: Players must feel risk-taking is appropriately rewarded
