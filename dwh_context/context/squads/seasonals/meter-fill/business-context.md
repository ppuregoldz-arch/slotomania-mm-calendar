# Meter-Fill Seasonals - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Meter-Fill Seasonals** represent seasonal features that require players to fill progress meters through bet or win-based activities before gaining entry to the main seasonal content. This entry mechanism creates engagement with designated machines, builds anticipation for seasonal access, and provides strategic timing for seasonal launches.

## Core Mechanics

### Meter-Filling Requirements
- **Bet-Based Progression**: Meters filled through betting activity on designated machines
- **Win-Based Progression**: Alternative progression through achieving wins or win amounts
- **Designated Machine Focus**: Specific machines become focal points during meter-fill periods
- **Entry Threshold**: Once meter is filled, players gain access to seasonal minigame or feature

### Progressive Entry System
- **Anticipation Building**: Meter-filling creates excitement and anticipation for seasonal access
- **Strategic Timing**: Players can choose when to focus on meter-filling activities
- **Multiple Entry Opportunities**: Some seasonals allow multiple meter fills for repeated access
- **Cross-Player Visibility**: Meter progress may be visible to create social dynamics

### Activity Integration
- **Core Gameplay Enhancement**: Meter-filling adds purpose to regular spinning and betting
- **Engagement Distribution**: Spreads player activity across designated machines
- **Session Length Extension**: Meter-filling requirements extend individual session duration
- **Strategic Machine Choice**: Players may alter machine selection based on meter-fill requirements

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Engagement Amplification**: Meter requirements increase overall gameplay engagement
- **Machine Traffic Direction**: Guide player activity to specific machines or game areas
- **Session Extension**: Meter-filling extends play sessions and total engagement time
- **Seasonal Launch Coordination**: Control seasonal access timing and player readiness

### Strategic Value
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Meter-filling connects core gameplay with seasonal content
- **Player Investment**: Time invested in meter-filling increases commitment to seasonal participation
- **Traffic Management**: Direct player focus to underutilized machines or content
- **Launch Strategy**: Coordinated meter-filling creates synchronized seasonal launches

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Meter-Fill Performance
- **Meter Completion Rate**: % of players who complete meter-fill requirements
- **Time to Completion**: Average time required to fill meters for seasonal access
- **Drop-off Analysis**: Where during meter-filling players typically disengage
- **Multi-Fill Engagement**: Players who complete multiple meter fills for repeated access

### Activity Impact Metrics
- **Designated Machine Engagement**: Increased activity on specified machines during meter-fill periods
- **Session Length Impact**: How meter requirements affect overall session duration
- **Cross-Machine Traffic**: Player machine selection changes during meter-fill periods
- **Engagement Distribution**: How meter requirements spread activity across game areas

### Entry Conversion Metrics
- **Fill-to-Entry Rate**: % of completed meter fills that result in seasonal participation
- **Entry Quality**: Engagement depth of players who enter via meter-fill vs other methods
- **Seasonal Preparation**: How meter-filling affects seasonal performance and spending
- **Cross-Feature Impact**: Meter-fill engagement effect on other game areas

## Technical Architecture

### Meter Progress Systems
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Live meter progress monitoring and display
- **Activity Attribution**: Recording which activities contribute to meter advancement
- **Threshold Management**: Entry gate activation when meter requirements are met
- **Progress Persistence**: Meter advancement saved across sessions until completion

### Machine Integration
- **Designated Machine Configuration**: Specifying which machines contribute to meter progress
- **Activity Weighting**: Different activities may contribute different amounts to meter progress
- **Progress Rate Balancing**: Calibrating meter fill requirements with desired completion times
- **Cross-Machine Coordination**: Managing meter progress across multiple machine types

## Feature Integration

### Core Gameplay Integration
- **Spinning Enhancement**: Regular gameplay becomes seasonal preparation activity
- **Machine Strategy**: Meter requirements influence player machine selection and strategy
- **Session Planning**: Players plan gameplay sessions around meter-fill objectives
- **Engagement Bridging**: Meter-filling connects core gameplay with seasonal excitement

### Seasonal Framework Integration
- **Launch Coordination**: Meter-fill completion coordinates with seasonal launch timing
- **Access Control**: Meter requirements manage seasonal entry and participation flow
- **Preparation Phase**: Meter-filling serves as seasonal preparation and anticipation period
- **Cross-Seasonal Balance**: Meter requirements coordinated across different seasonal types

## Risk Areas & Monitoring

### Meter Balance
- **Completion Time Calibration**: Ensure meter requirements are achievable within desired timeframes
- **Difficulty Accessibility**: Balance meter requirements to be challenging but accessible
- **Drop-off Prevention**: Avoid meter requirements that cause excessive player disengagement

### Activity Impact
- **Machine Traffic Balance**: Ensure meter requirements don't over-concentrate activity inappropriately
- **Cross-Feature Disruption**: Monitor that meter-filling doesn't negatively affect other game areas
- **Session Health**: Balance meter requirements with healthy session length and enjoyment

### Player Experience
- **Anticipation vs Frustration**: Ensure meter-filling builds excitement rather than creates obstacles
- **Progress Clarity**: Players must understand meter requirements and progress clearly
- **Reward Justification**: Seasonal access must justify time and effort invested in meter-filling
