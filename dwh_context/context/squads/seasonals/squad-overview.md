# Seasonals Squad Overview

## Squad Mission
The Seasonals squad focuses on **short-term rotating features** (typically 5-7 days) that provide themed surprise content aligned with monthly/album cycles. These features create excitement through novelty, drive engagement through meter-filling mechanics, and generate incremental revenue through direct-purchase products like extra picks and special items.

## Key Business Areas
- **Content Freshness**: Regular 5-7 day content rotation preventing player fatigue
- **Themed Engagement**: Monthly/album-aligned content creating cohesive player experience
- **Surprise & Discovery**: Rotating surprise features maintaining game excitement
- **Direct Monetization**: Extra picks and premium items driving incremental revenue
- **Meter-Fill Mechanics**: Bet/win-based progression creating engagement and spending incentives
- **Short-Term Goals**: Weekly completion objectives providing immediate play motivation

## Seasonal Feature Framework
- **Duration**: Typically 5-7 days per seasonal cycle
- **Themed Content**: Aligned with monthly themes and current album seasons
- **Entry Mechanics**: Most require meter filling through bet or win-based activities
- **Direct Revenue**: Extra picks, special items, and premium progression options
- **Surprise Elements**: Rotating mechanics keep features feeling fresh and unpredictable
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Seasonal rewards often include cards, coins, gems, and other cross-feature benefits
