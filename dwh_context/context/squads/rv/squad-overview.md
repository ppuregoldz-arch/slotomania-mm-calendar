# RV Squad - Overview

**Squad**: Rewarded Video (RV)
**Focus**: Video ad monetization and user engagement through rewarded ad experiences

## Squad Structure

### Sub-Features
- **[Shiny Show](shiny-show/)** - Strategic ad placement during specific game moments
- **General RV** - Core rewarded video functionality (see general-rv.md)

### Key Areas
- Video ad serving and revenue optimization
- User segmentation and targeting
- A/B testing and configuration management
- Revenue threshold and eCPM optimization
- Ad priority and fill rate monitoring
