# Winovate - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Winovate** is a mid-term renovation feature where players spend hammers to renovate items across different scenes, participate in Hammer Strike minigames, and earn hammers from designated games. The feature combines renovation progression with mini-game elements and strategic resource management.

## Core Mechanics

### Renovation System
- **Scene-Based Renovation**: Multiple scenes with different renovation projects and themes
- **Hammer Currency**: Hammers serve as the primary currency for renovation activities
- **Item Renovation**: Individual items within scenes can be renovated using hammer spending
- **Progressive Renovation**: Scenes unlock and progress through renovation completion

### Hammer Strike Minigame
- **Mini-game Integration**: Hammer Strike provides interactive gameplay within renovation feature
- **Hammer Earning**: Minigame participation generates hammers for renovation projects
- **Skill-Based Elements**: Player interaction affects hammer earning efficiency
- **Engagement Enhancement**: Minigame adds variety to standard collection mechanics

### Hammer Economy
- **Designated Game Earning**: Hammers earned through play on specific designated machines
- **Multiple Earning Channels**: Hammers available through various gameplay activities
- **Strategic Spending**: Players choose which renovation projects to prioritize
- **Resource Management**: Balance hammer earning and spending across scenes

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Creative Engagement**: Renovation theme appeals to players interested in building/improvement
- **Resource Management**: Hammer economy creates strategic decision-making
- **Mini-game Variety**: Hammer Strike adds interactive gameplay diversity
- **Progressive Achievement**: Scene renovation provides clear completion goals

### Strategic Value
- **Engagement Differentiation**: Renovation mechanics distinct from collection-only features
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Designated machine play enhanced by hammer earning
- **Strategic Depth**: Resource management and scene prioritization create gameplay sophistication
- **Completion Satisfaction**: Renovation completion provides visible achievement and transformation

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Renovation Metrics
- **Scene Completion Rate**: % of participants completing full scene renovations
- **Item Renovation Distribution**: Player focus across different renovation items
- **Hammer Spending Patterns**: Resource allocation across scenes and projects
- **Cross-Scene Engagement**: Players working on multiple scenes simultaneously

### Mini-game Metrics
- **Hammer Strike Participation**: % of Winovate players engaging with minigame
- **Minigame Session Length**: Average time spent in Hammer Strike activities
- **Hammer Earning Efficiency**: Hammer generation rate through minigame participation
- **Repeat Engagement**: Player return rate to Hammer Strike minigame

### Resource Management Metrics
- **Hammer Earning Rate**: Collection velocity through designated games and minigames
- **Spending Strategy Analysis**: Player prioritization patterns across renovation options
- **Resource Utilization**: Efficiency of hammer spending vs. earning balance
- **Completion Timeline**: Average time from feature start to scene completion
