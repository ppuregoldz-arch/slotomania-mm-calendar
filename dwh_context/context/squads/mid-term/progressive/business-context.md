# Progressive Systems - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Progressive Systems** encompass various mid-term features that utilize progressive mechanics including bar progression, dynamic jackpots, participation-based systems, and ante-bet micro features. These systems create escalating rewards and engagement through incremental advancement and milestone achievement.

## Core Progressive Mechanics

### Bar Progression Systems
- **Progressive Bars**: Incremental filling through various gameplay activities
- **Milestone Rewards**: Rewards distributed at progression checkpoints
- **Multi-Stage Progression**: Multiple bars or stages with increasing reward values
- **Reset Cycles**: Periodic bar resets with fresh progression opportunities

### Dynamic Jackpot Systems
- **Escalating Jackpots**: Progressive jackpot values that increase with participation
- **Ante-Bet Integration**: Player betting behavior influences jackpot progression
- **PU vs NPU Segmentation**: Different jackpot experiences for paying vs non-paying users
- **Payout Analysis**: Comprehensive jackpot distribution and winner tracking

### Participation Features
- **Community Progression**: Shared progression goals across player base
- **Individual Contribution**: Personal participation affecting community objectives
- **Collaborative Rewards**: Rewards distributed based on community achievement
- **Engagement Amplification**: Community goals driving individual participation

### Ante-Bet Micro Systems
- **Micro-Betting Integration**: Small additional bet options with progressive rewards
- **Betting Percentiles**: Player segmentation based on betting behavior patterns
- **Risk-Reward Calibration**: Progressive reward scaling with betting commitment
- **Behavioral Analytics**: Detailed analysis of micro-betting participation patterns
