# General Clans - Business Context & Core Mechanics

**Feature**: Core Clans System
**Purpose**: Social gameplay foundation with community engagement and collaborative progression
**Category**: Social Features & Community Building

## Feature Overview

### Core Social Mechanics
- **Social Groups**: Players join clans for collaborative gameplay and social interaction
- **Communal Challenges**: Shared objectives and goals that require clan coordination
- **Clan Points**: Currency system for clan-level progression and rewards
- **Chest Rewards**: Collaborative reward system based on clan performance
- **Trading System**: Inter-player asset exchange within clan ecosystem
- **Chat System**: Real-time communication platform for clan coordination

### Clan Infrastructure
- **Clan Badges**: Achievement system that feeds into "Clan Go" progress bar
- **Lobby Integration**: Permanent clan icon in main lobby for easy access
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Clan elements embedded across multiple game features
- **Social Progression**: Clan-based advancement systems and tier progression

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Retention Enhancement**: Social bonds increase user stickiness and lifetime value
- **Engagement Amplification**: Collaborative features drive higher session frequency and duration
- **Monetization Through Social Pressure**: Clan-based purchasing drivers and group dynamics
- **Community Building**: Foster long-term player communities and social networks

### Strategic Integration
- **Cross-Feature Synergy**: Clan elements integrated across albums, daily dash, seasonals
- **Social Monetization**: Leverage group dynamics for purchase motivation
- **Retention Infrastructure**: Social connections as primary retention mechanism
- **User Generated Content**: Player-driven clan culture and identity formation

### Key Risks
- **Toxic Behavior**: Negative social interactions affecting user experience

- **Economic Imbalance**: Unfair reward distribution creating clan inequality
- **Technical Scalability**: Chat, trading, and social systems performance under load

### Mitigation Strategies
- **Moderation Systems**: Automated and human moderation for chat and social interactions
- **Clan Health Monitoring**: Automated systems to identify and address inactive clans
- **Economic Balancing**: Regular analysis and adjustment of clan point economy
- **Scalable Infrastructure**: Robust technical architecture for social feature growth
