# General VIP Context

## Inner Circle Overview

### Eligibility and Access
**Tier Requirement**: Players must reach Platinum tier to automatically become VIP players and access Inner Circle.

**Platform Access**:
- Web: www.Slotomania.com
- Mobile: VIP/PWA apps on iOS and Android devices
- Landing page: slotomaniavip.com for app installation and benefit details

**Membership Verification**: Benefits visible in Personal Profile under "Membership Benefits" when accessed via .com or VIP apps.

Enhanced medal spin rewards:
- **Bronze**: 5 spins (vs 3 standard)

#### Seasonal Feature Benefits
- **Blast**: 2 extra picks with every Pick offer

- **Safety Net**: Weekly compensation for net losses

  - Cap: 3x median bet (10x if purchased during week)
  - Distribution: Every Tuesday, based on Monday 12:00 UTC to Monday 12:00 UTC

- **Ace Machine**: More spins when winning Ace Cards (varies by album)
