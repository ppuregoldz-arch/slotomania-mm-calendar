# Dice Deluxe - Business Context

**Feature**: Dice Deluxe (Premium Dice Upgrade)
**Purpose**: Premium upgrade to the free Dice feature that provides enhanced multipliers for Special Bonus collection
**Trigger**: Available after landing x150 multiplier on free Dice

## Feature Overview

Dice is a **Special Bonus multiplier system** that enhances the core freemium experience through chance-based rewards. The feature has two tiers: free Dice (always available) and premium Dice Deluxe (purchasable upgrade).

### Special Bonus Foundation
- **Core Mechanic**: Players collect Special Bonus for free every predefined number of hours
- **Purchase Benefit**: Paying users can collect Special Bonus every hour (reduced cooldown)
- **Foundation Role**: Special Bonus serves as the foundation for many freemium features
- **Dice Trigger**: Every Special Bonus collection triggers a Dice roll

### Free Dice Mechanics
- **Activation**: Triggered automatically with every Special Bonus collection
- **Function**: Multiplies Special Bonus value by the die result
- **Die Faces**: Six possible outcomes
  - x1 (standard)
  - x2 (2x multiplier)
  - x3 (3x multiplier)  
  - x4 (4x multiplier)
  - x5 (5x multiplier)
  - x150 (jackpot multiplier - unlocks Dice Deluxe availability)

### Dice Deluxe Premium Upgrade
- **Unlock Condition**: Available only after landing x150 on free Dice
- **Purchase Model**: One-time premium upgrade purchase
- **Enhanced Rewards**: Significantly higher multipliers than free version
- **Target Audience**: Players who experienced the x150 jackpot and want consistent high multipliers

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Monetize Special Bonus Experience**: Convert free players to paid through enhanced rewards
- **Increase Special Bonus Engagement**: Create excitement and anticipation around collection
- **Drive Purchase Conversion**: Use x150 jackpot as a "taste of premium" to drive Dice Deluxe sales
- **Enhance Player Value**: Provide meaningful upgrade to core freemium mechanic

### Secondary Benefits
- **Retention Tool**: Enhanced Special Bonus value keeps players engaged with core collection loop
- **Segmentation Opportunity**: Identify high-engagement players through x150 hits
- **Cross-Feature Support**: Enhanced Special Bonus values benefit all features built on this foundation

## Key Performance Indicators

### Conversion Metrics
- **Dice Deluxe Conversion Rate**: % of x150 hits that convert to Dice Deluxe purchase
- **Special Bonus Collection Rate**: Frequency of Special Bonus collection with/without Dice
- **Multiplier Distribution**: Frequency of each die face outcome in free vs premium version

### Engagement Metrics  
- **Daily Special Bonus Collections**: Average collections per player per day
- **Dice Roll Frequency**: Total dice rolls (correlated with Special Bonus engagement)
- **Purchase Benefit Adoption**: % of paying users using hourly Special Bonus collection

### Revenue Impact
- **Dice Deluxe Revenue**: Direct revenue from premium upgrades
- **Special Bonus Value Enhancement**: Increased coin/reward value through multipliers
- **Cross-Feature Revenue Impact**: Revenue impact on features built on Special Bonus foundation

## Feature Integration

### Special Bonus Ecosystem
- **Core Collection**: Base Special Bonus collection (free every X hours, paid every hour)
- **Dice Multiplier**: Applied to every collection (free 1x-150x, premium enhanced range)
- **Downstream Features**: Enhanced Special Bonus feeds into other freemium features
- **Purchase Benefit Synergy**: Premium users get both reduced cooldown AND better dice

### Dynamic Sources System
- **Trigger Sources**: Multiple sources can trigger free Dice (Special Bonus collection being primary)
- **Source Classification**: Dynamic categorization system for different trigger types
- **Flexibility**: Sources can change over time based on business needs and feature evolution
- **Analytics Tracking**: Source identification critical for performance analysis

## User Journey

### Free Player Experience
1. **Special Bonus Collection**: Collect every X hours for free
2. **Automatic Dice Roll**: Every collection triggers free Dice roll (1x-150x)
3. **Multiplied Rewards**: Special Bonus value multiplied by die result
4. **Jackpot Moment**: Landing x150 unlocks Dice Deluxe availability

### Premium Player Experience (Dice Deluxe)
1. **Enhanced Collection**: Special Bonus every hour (if Purchase Benefit owner)
2. **Premium Dice**: Higher multiplier ranges than free version
3. **Consistent Value**: More reliable high multipliers vs. free version randomness
4. **Compound Benefits**: Enhanced Special Bonus fuels all dependent features

## Analytical Focus Areas

### Performance Analysis
- **Die Face Distribution**: Ensure fair randomization and proper multiplier weighting
- **Conversion Funnel**: Track journey from x150 hit to Dice Deluxe purchase
- **Source Performance**: Analyze which trigger sources drive best engagement/conversion

### User Behavior Analysis
- **Collection Patterns**: How Dice affects Special Bonus collection frequency
- **Premium vs Free Usage**: Behavior differences between Dice versions
- **Cross-Feature Impact**: How enhanced Special Bonus affects other feature engagement

### Revenue Optimization
- **Pricing Strategy**: Optimal Dice Deluxe price point based on x150 conversion rates
- **Multiplier Tuning**: Balance between free dice excitement and premium upgrade value
- **Source Strategy**: Which trigger sources drive best monetization outcomes

---
**Note**: Dice creates a sophisticated risk-reward layer over the foundational Special Bonus system, using the psychology of variable rewards (slot machine effect) and premium upgrades to monetize core freemium engagement while maintaining the free player experience.
