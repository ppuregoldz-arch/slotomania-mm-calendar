# Boosters - Queries & Analytics

## Summary
This file contains analytical patterns and dashboard intelligence for booster performance, conversion tracking, and revenue attribution within the Purchase Tools ecosystem.

## Key Analytical Areas

### Conversion Analysis
- **Dice Deluxe Conversion Tracking**: x150 landing to purchase conversion rates by user segment
- **Booster Adoption Rates**: First-time booster purchase patterns and user characteristics
- **Repeat Purchase Behavior**: Multi-purchase patterns and player lifecycle analysis
- **Revenue Attribution**: Direct and indirect revenue impact of booster availability

### User Behavior Analytics
- **Special Bonus Collection Patterns**: How boosters affect collection frequency and timing
- **Session Impact Analysis**: Session length and engagement changes with booster usage
- **Feature Discovery**: How players learn about and adopt booster functionality
- **Cross-Feature Correlation**: Booster usage correlation with other purchase behaviors

### Performance Segmentation
- **User Segment Performance**: Booster effectiveness across different player segments (NPU, DPU, etc.)
- **Geographic Analysis**: Regional differences in booster adoption and conversion
- **Platform Comparison**: Booster performance differences across platforms
- **Timing Analysis**: Optimal moments for booster offers and purchases

### Revenue Impact
- **ARPBU Calculation**: Average Revenue Per Booster User tracking
- **LTV Enhancement**: Lifetime value impact of booster adoption
- **Revenue Mix Analysis**: Booster revenue as percentage of total Purchase Tools revenue
- **ROI Measurement**: Return on investment for booster development and marketing
