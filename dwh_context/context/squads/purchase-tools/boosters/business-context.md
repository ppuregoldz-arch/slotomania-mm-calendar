# Boosters - Business Context

## Overview
Boosters are purchasable enhancements that modify or amplify specific game mechanics, primarily focused on the Special Bonus system. They provide temporary or permanent improvements to player rewards and gameplay experience.

## Core Booster Systems

### Star Dice Booster
**Primary Function**: Enhances the Special Bonus collection experience through an upgraded dice system.

**Mechanics**:
- **Standard Dice Faces**: x1, x2, x3, x4, x5, x150
- **Activation**: Automatically triggers when collecting Special Bonus with booster active
- **Premium Upgrade**: When landing on x150 multiplier, unlocks Dice Deluxe purchase option
- **Distribution**: Previously available in dedicated store section, now integrated into Special Bonus flow

### Dice System Integration
**Free Dice (Base Game)**:
- Rolls automatically on Special Bonus collection
- 6-face die with multipliers affecting Special Bonus value
- Landing on x150 enables premium upgrade opportunity

**Dice Deluxe (Premium Version)**:
- Purchasable upgrade after landing x150 on free dice
- Significantly higher multipliers than standard dice
- Enhanced reward potential for Special Bonus collections

### Booster Purchase Flow
**Acquisition Method**:
- Integrated into Special Bonus collection sequence
- Triggered by specific dice outcomes (x150 result)
- Direct purchase opportunities during optimal moments

**Targeting Strategy**:
- Offered when player experiences high-value free result
- Capitalizes on positive momentum and engagement
- Provides immediate upgrade path for enhanced rewards

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Monetize Special Bonus System**: Convert free reward moments into purchase opportunities
- **Enhance Player Experience**: Provide meaningful upgrades to core game mechanics
- **Increase Session Value**: Boost per-session revenue through booster purchases
- **Create Purchase Momentum**: Use positive outcomes to drive additional spending

### Secondary Goals
- **Player Retention**: Enhanced rewards create stickier gameplay experience
- **Feature Discovery**: Introduce players to premium upgrade concepts
- **Revenue Diversification**: Additional monetization layer beyond direct coin/gem purchases

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Conversion Metrics
- **Dice Deluxe Conversion Rate**: % of x150 landings that convert to purchase
- **Booster Attach Rate**: % of Special Bonus collections with active booster
- **Repeat Purchase Rate**: % of players who purchase boosters multiple times

### Revenue Metrics
- **Average Booster Revenue per User (ARPBU)**
- **Booster Revenue as % of Total Revenue**
- **Lifetime Value Impact**: LTV difference for booster vs non-booster users

### Engagement Metrics
- **Special Bonus Collection Frequency**: Impact of boosters on collection patterns
- **Session Length Extension**: How boosters affect overall session duration
- **Feature Adoption Rate**: % of eligible players who try boosters

## Technical Architecture

### Integration Points
- **Special Bonus System**: Core integration for automatic triggering
- **Payment Flow**: Seamless purchase experience during gameplay
- **Inventory Management**: Booster activation and duration tracking
- **Analytics Integration**: Comprehensive conversion and usage tracking

### User Experience Flow
1. **Collection Trigger**: Player collects Special Bonus
2. **Dice Roll**: Automatic dice roll with current booster (if active)
3. **Result Processing**: Multiplier applied to Special Bonus value
4. **Upgrade Opportunity**: x150 result triggers Dice Deluxe offer
5. **Purchase Flow**: Optional upgrade purchase with immediate activation

## Ecosystem Integration

### Feature Synergy
- **Special Bonus Optimization**: Core system that drives booster value proposition
- **Payment Integration**: Seamless with existing purchase infrastructure
- **Gem Economy**: Alternative currency option for booster purchases
- **VIP Benefits**: Enhanced booster effectiveness for Inner Circle players

### Cross-Feature Impact
- **Daily Engagement**: Boosters encourage more frequent Special Bonus collection
- **Monetization Ecosystem**: Complements other purchase tools and offers
- **Player Progression**: Enhanced rewards accelerate overall game progression

## Risk Areas

### Player Experience Risks
- **Over-Monetization**: Risk of making core feature feel too commercial
- **Fairness Perception**: Ensuring non-paying players still have engaging experience
- **Purchase Pressure**: Avoiding aggressive or manipulative selling tactics

### Technical Risks
- **Integration Complexity**: Ensuring seamless operation with Special Bonus system
- **Performance Impact**: Minimal effect on game performance and loading
- **Data Accuracy**: Proper tracking of booster effects and purchase attribution

### Business Risks
- **Cannibalization**: Risk of reducing other monetization channel effectiveness
- **Feature Fatigue**: Players becoming overwhelmed by purchase opportunities
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensuring transparent and fair booster mechanics

## Market Positioning
- **Premium Enhancement**: Positioned as valuable upgrade rather than necessity
- **Opportunistic Monetization**: Leverages positive player moments for conversion
- **Complementary Revenue**: Adds to existing monetization without replacement
- **Player Choice**: Maintains strong free-to-play experience while offering premium options
