# In-Game Currencies - Business Context

## Overview
Slotomania employs a multi-currency ecosystem designed to create diverse monetization opportunities, enhance player engagement, and provide flexible reward mechanisms across various game features.

## Primary Currencies

### Coins (Base Currency)
**Function**: Core gameplay currency used for spinning slot machines and basic game progression.

**Acquisition**:
- Starting balance: 15 million coins for new players at level 1
- Purchases from Coin Store (primary monetization)
- Daily bonuses and lobby rewards
- Machine winnings and jackpots
- Free gifts and promotional offers
- Social features (clan rewards, friend gifts)

**Usage**:
- Slot machine betting (all denominations)
- Machine-specific features and bonus buys
- Some promotional offers and special events

### Gems (Premium Currency)
**Function**: Primary premium currency for enhanced gameplay experiences and feature acceleration.

**Acquisition Sources**:
- Direct purchase from Gems Store (main monetization path)
- Ace Machine spins (random rewards)
- Shiny Tree discoveries (limited opportunities)
- Seasonal events (Blast, Holey Moley board rewards)
- Clan Chests (social rewards)
- In-app offers when balance insufficient

**Core Usage Categories**:

#### Feature Enhancements
- **Daily Dash**: Extra spin redemption
- **Shiny Show**: Additional picks and continues
- **SlotoQuest**: Power-up activation (5-7 minutes, doubles progress)
- **Hidden Objects**: Extra time purchases
- **Snakes & Ladders**: Shield protection for dice rolls
- **Smash 'N' Grab**: Continue after failed attempts

#### Machine-Specific Features
- **Happy Paws Machine**: Pet bundles with guaranteed rare items
- **Civilitreasures/Farm Fortune**: Guard/Dog purchases (removes spin limitations when <10% spins remain)
- **Time to Ca$h**: Super Win save feature (time travel replay)
- **69 Fun Machine**: Upgrade activations during Game Mania/Event Days
- **Astrology INC**: Game Mania and event day activations
- **Get Me a Prince**: Great Choices purchases (premium dialogue options)

#### System Extensions
- **Gift Cards**: Extend storage limit beyond standard cap
- **Various Features**: Emergency continues and premium options

**Gem Purchase Benefits**:
- 1-hour Power boost
- SlotoCards rewards
- Status Points accumulation
- Club Points earning
- **Note**: Gem purchases exclude coins, making them ineligible for stamps, gift cards, and coin-value promotions

### Slotobucks (Evolution Currency)
**System Evolution**: Underwent major revision in May 2025 with selective rollout.

#### Legacy Slotobucks (Pre-May 2025)
- Form of internal currency earned as cashback from real-money purchases
- Converted to coins during new system implementation

#### New Slotobucks (May 2025+)
**Acquisition**:
- Real-money purchase cashback (primary method)
- Promotional rewards and special offers
- Future expansion into additional earning methods

**Visibility**:
- **Stash Integration**: Appears in player's seasonal token storage
- **Store Display**: Visible at top of store interface for easy access

**Usage Applications**:
- **Dedicated Store Section**: Exclusive Slotobucks marketplace
- **Specialized Machines**: Select machines accept Slotobucks betting

**Current Slotobucks-Enabled Machines**:
- Tex Tycoon's Oil Boom
- Panning For Gold  
- Catching Hook
- King Olmec

**Rollout Strategy**:
- **Phased Implementation**: Two test groups with staggered communication and conversion
- **Group 1 (Test_1)**: Communication May 25th, conversion May 27th
- **Group 2 (Test_2)**: Communication May 26th, conversion May 28th
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Dedicated Tableau tracking for performance monitoring

## Secondary/Seasonal Currencies

### Hero Coins (Feature-Specific)
- **Globez Coins**: Used in Globez seasonal feature
- **Figz Currency**: SlotoFigz feature progression
- **Reset Cycle**: All hero coins reset at season end
- **Backoffice Tracking**: Monitored under "Hero Coins" filter in In-Game Currency tab

### Pet Points
- **Source**: Happy Paws Machine gameplay
- **Usage**: Pet bundle purchases (alternative to gems)
- **Machine Integration**: Specific to pet-themed features

## Business Strategy

### Monetization Framework
- **Coins**: High-volume, frequent purchases for continuous gameplay
- **Gems**: Premium experiences and convenience monetization
- **Slotobucks**: Loyalty rewards and cashback retention mechanism
- **Hero Coins**: Feature-specific engagement and seasonal retention

### Currency Separation Benefits
- **Psychological Pricing**: Different currencies create varied value perceptions
- **Feature Gating**: Premium experiences accessible through specific currencies
- **Spending Diversification**: Multiple purchase motivations and price points
- **Retention Mechanics**: Cashback currencies encourage continued engagement

## Technical Architecture

### Currency Management
- **Real-Time Balance Tracking**: Instant updates across all features
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Seamless currency usage across game systems
- **Purchase Flow Integration**: Automatic insufficient balance handling with purchase offers
- **Analytics Integration**: Comprehensive tracking of earning, spending, and conversion patterns

### Security Measures
- **Server-Side Validation**: All currency transactions validated server-side
- **Anti-Fraud Systems**: Protection against currency manipulation
- **Transaction Logging**: Complete audit trail for all currency movements
- **Balance Recovery**: Systems for resolving currency discrepancies

## Risk Areas

### Economic Balance
- **Inflation Management**: Preventing currency devaluation through oversupply
- **Conversion Rate Optimization**: Balancing free vs. paid currency acquisition
- **Feature Pricing**: Ensuring gem costs remain attractive vs. alternative options

### Player Experience
- **Currency Confusion**: Managing complexity of multiple currency systems
- **Purchase Pressure**: Avoiding aggressive monetization that harms F2P experience
- **Value Perception**: Maintaining clear value propositions for each currency

### Technical Risks
- **Synchronization Issues**: Ensuring currency balances remain accurate across sessions
- **Conversion Accuracy**: Proper handling of currency exchanges and upgrades
- **Feature Integration**: Seamless operation across all currency-enabled features

## Future Evolution
- **New Slotobucks Expansion**: Potential additional earning methods and usage applications
- **Hero Coins Integration**: Possible cross-seasonal currency systems
- **Purchase Tools Integration**: Enhanced integration with other monetization features
- **Analytics Enhancement**: Improved tracking and optimization of currency ecosystems
