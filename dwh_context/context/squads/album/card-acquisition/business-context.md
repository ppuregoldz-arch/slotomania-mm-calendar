# Card Acquisition - Business Context

## Feature Overview
**Card Acquisition** encompasses all mechanics and channels through which players obtain collectible cards for their albums. This system balances free earning opportunities with monetization pathways, creating sustainable engagement while driving revenue through strategic scarcity and completion urgency.

## Core Mechanics

### Acquisition Channels
- **Gameplay Rewards**: Cards earned through regular spins, bonuses, and progression
- **Daily Dash Integration**: Cards as challenge completion rewards and progression milestones
- **Purchase Channels**: Direct card pack purchases via Payment Page and Gem Store
- **Challenge Systems**: Cards from seasonal features, mini-games, and special events
- **Gift Distribution**: Promotional card grants and special campaigns
- **Wild Card Acquisition**: Universal completion cards from premium channels

### Card Pack Economics
- **Pack Structure**: Multiple cards per purchase with rarity distribution
- **Value Proposition**: Enhanced card acquisition rate vs. natural gameplay earning
- **Rarity Guarantees**: Minimum rare card guarantees in purchased packs
- **Bonus Inclusions**: Additional value items (coins, gems, stamps) in card packs

### Wild Card System
- **Universal Application**: Wild cards can complete any missing album slot
- **Premium Positioning**: Primarily available through gem purchases and premium rewards
- **Strategic Usage**: Players save wild cards for hardest-to-acquire missing cards
- **Economy Balance**: Limited availability maintains collection challenge

## Business Goals

### Primary Objectives
- **Conversion Optimization**: Transform collection desire into purchase behavior
- **Engagement Sustainability**: Balance free earning with purchase incentives
- **Channel Distribution**: Spread card acquisition across multiple game features
- **Completion Facilitation**: Provide paths to album completion for different spending levels

### Strategic Value
- **Revenue Generation**: Direct monetization through card pack and wild card sales
- **Feature Integration**: Drive engagement with other game systems through card rewards
- **Player Retention**: Acquisition goals provide daily play motivation
- **Spending Distribution**: Multiple price points accommodate different player budgets

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Acquisition Metrics
- **Cards per Source**: Distribution of card acquisition across channels
- **Purchase Conversion Rate**: % of card viewers who make acquisition purchases
- **Wild Card Usage Rate**: Efficiency of premium wild card utilization
- **Pack Opening Frequency**: Engagement with card pack opening experience

### Channel Performance
- **Gameplay Earning Rate**: Free card acquisition velocity through standard play
- **Daily Dash Card Rewards**: Card distribution effectiveness through challenges
- **Purchase Channel Efficiency**: Revenue per card pack sold by channel
- **Event Card Distribution**: Special acquisition during seasonal features

### Monetization Metrics
- **Card Pack Revenue**: Direct revenue from card acquisition purchases
- **Average Pack Value**: Revenue per card pack transaction
- **Wild Card Gem Spending**: Gem economy contribution from universal cards
- **Acquisition-to-Completion Rate**: % of purchasers who complete albums

## Technical Architecture

### Data Tracking
- **Card Source Attribution**: Every card acquisition tagged with source channel
- **Pack Opening Events**: Detailed logging of purchased pack contents
- **Wild Card Application**: Usage tracking for universal completion cards
- **Acquisition Journey**: Player path from card discovery to acquisition

### Integration Systems
- **Payment Integration**: Seamless card pack purchases through existing payment systems
- **Inventory Management**: Real-time card ownership and completion status tracking
- **Reward Distribution**: Automated card grants from challenges and events
- **Cross-feature Coordination**: Card rewards integrated into other game features

## Risk Areas & Monitoring

### Acquisition Balance
- **Free vs. Paid Balance**: Ensure viable free-to-play progression path
- **Channel Effectiveness**: Monitor all acquisition channels for player engagement
- **Wild Card Economy**: Balance universal card availability with collection challenge

### Player Experience
- **Acquisition Frustration**: Prevent excessive difficulty in card earning
- **Purchase Pressure**: Maintain positive experience around monetization
- **Collection Fairness**: Ensure reasonable completion paths for all player types
