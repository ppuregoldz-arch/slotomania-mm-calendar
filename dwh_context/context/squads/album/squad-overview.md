# Album Squad Overview

## Squad Mission
The Album squad focuses on **card collection and progression mechanics** — analyzing player engagement with album completion, card acquisition patterns, revenue generation through card packs and wild cards, and the overall card collection ecosystem that drives long-term retention and monetization in Slotomania.

## Key Sub-Features

### 1. **General Album System**
- **Navigation**: `context/squads/album/general-album.md`
- **Core album collection mechanics, card progression, and completion rewards**

### 2. **Card Acquisition**
- **Navigation**: `context/squads/album/card-acquisition/`
- **Card pack purchases, wild card usage, missing card mechanics**

### 3. **Album Completion & Rewards**
- **Navigation**: `context/squads/album/album-completion/`
- **Completion bonuses, revenue progression, finisher performance**

### 4. **Fusion Albums**
- **Navigation**: `context/squads/album/fusion-albums/`

- **Navigation**: `context/squads/album/revenue/`
- **Gem usage, card purchase revenue, monetization analytics**

## Key Business Areas
- **Card Collection Engagement**: Completion rates, progression patterns, retention impact
- **Monetization**: Gem spending on card packs, wild card purchases, revenue per album
- **Missing Card Strategy**: Near-miss mechanics, completion urgency, purchase conversion
- **Album Performance**: Revenue progression per set, finisher lifetime value
- **Cross-Feature Integration**: Album interaction with Seasonals, Daily Dash, Purchase Tools
