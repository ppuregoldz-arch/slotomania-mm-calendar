# Promotion Glossary for Creative Briefs

**Last updated:** July 2026
**Source:** Direct clarifications from Itay.

## August 1 promotion definitions

### ADS Personal Offer — Coins

- `PO` means **Personal Offer**, not Purchase Offer.
- Selected users watch an ad and then receive the prize; there is no purchase.
- Coins quantities are dynamic. A quantity change alone remains **Reuse**.

### Shiny Show — Joker All Cards

- Shiny Show is an elevator game with four hidden boxes per floor.
- A chosen box gives its prize or reveals Moley, who takes the accumulated prizes unless the player pays Gems.
- **Joker** is a mechanic: landing on a Joker awards all prizes on that floor.
- **All Cards** means all prizes are cards; it does not mean every revealed card is a Joker.
- The August 1 version is **Reuse** because its prizes match the prior Joker All Cards execution exactly.
- A different visible reward set with the same Shiny Show structure is **Prize Change**.

### Custom Pod — X1000

- Generic promotional art, unrelated to a specific event.
- `X1000` is a maximum prize of 1,000 times the player's bet.
- Changing the multiplier is **Prize Change**.

### Win Master — 3★ Regular Card + PAB

- The mission is to win a configured number of times; both listed rewards are granted.
- `PAB` means **Pick a Boom**.
- Win Master now runs through **M.E.S — Mission Event System**.
- Always open the complete M.E.S Creative asset set, even when recent historical work changed only a Banner.

### Daily Deal — 3★ Regular Card + Hammers Wheel

- The purchase includes both rewards together.
- If the same combination and art already exist, use **Reuse**; changing either visible reward is **Prize Change**.

### Status Boost

- Players receive the next tier's benefits plus bigger Coin packages.
- A changed benefit/boost percentage is **Prize Change**.

### First of Month — Biggest Store Denom

- A special largest package inside the regular store containing both Coins and Gems.
- First used for July 4 and adopted as a monthly guideline for the first day of each month.
- Values and art are decided case by case; verify each month before assigning Reuse or Prize Change.

### Lotto Peak

- Operational timing/peak row paired with Lotto Bonus Premium.
- It has no separate player-facing Creative work and must not receive a standalone Creative brief.

### Lotto Bonus Premium — Bigger Balls

- `LBP` means **Lotto Bonus Premium**.
- Bigger Balls increases the reward value of every ball by the stated percentage.
- Changing that percentage is **Prize Change**.

### Reveal Your Deal

- `RYD` means **Reveal Your Deal**.
- The player reveals one personalized package and always reveals the largest offer.
- Changing the visible package values is **Prize Change**.

### Cozy Blast — Wild Ordinary

- Cozy season and Wild Ordinary reward are one promo and one Creative brief, not two.
- `Cozy` is the visual theme.
- `Wild Ordinary` is a Wild card for the ordinary/regular album collection.
- Include both theme and reward in the title.

### Golden Spin

- **Not** Gem Machine, Happy Hour, or any timed **Gems** ALERT window. Golden Spin is the **premium Mega Bonus wheel** (coin-value wedges / variants such as Bigger Multipliers, Extra Black Wedge, themed arena).
- Briefs and parent **Change** use variant + optional **Theme** from the MM row; never paste MM boilerplate that calls it a “timed gem feature.”
- CRM3 lives under `Features\Golden_Spin\`; do not reuse Gem Machine / `Winning_Spins_Gems` folders unless Itay directs a one-off.

### Backup Coupon Status Boost

- A general revenue fallback if a main offer underperforms or is canceled; it is not limited to Status Boost failure.
- `PU` means **Paying Users**.
- `PRAS` means **Inner Circle**, whose players receive higher values.
- The 30%/50% uplift applies to Coins value.
- Audience/segment changes alone are **Reuse**.

## Label precedence reminder

`New promo` when the structure is new → otherwise `New theme for promo` when theme changes → otherwise `Prize Change` when art-visible content changes → otherwise `Reuse`.
